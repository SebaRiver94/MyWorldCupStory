Cities

During my trip to the 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar, I explored modern cities packed with culture and energy. From Doha's skyline to Lusail’s new districts and Al Khor’s coastal vibe, every place felt uniquely connected to the tournament.

Doha

The heart of the World Cup buzz and fan zones.

Lusail

Futuristic city built around the final venue.

Al Khor

Coastal town with a family-friendly atmosphere.

Al Rayyan

Home to several venues and lively matchdays.

Al Wakrah

Seaside vibes and modern public spaces.

Lusail Stadium

The iconic venue for the World Cup final.

Al Bayt Stadium

Tent-inspired design with a grand presence.

Al Janoub Stadium

Curved lines inspired by traditional dhows.

Education City Stadium

Geometric façade shining by night.

Ahmad Bin Ali Stadium

Patterned exterior reflecting desert dunes.

Al Thumama Stadium

Inspired by the traditional gahfiya cap.

Stadium 974

Modular design built with shipping containers.

Khalifa International Stadium

Qatar’s classic arena with World Cup upgrades.

Stadiums

Qatar’s World Cup stadiums blended cutting-edge design with strong cultural references. Watching games there felt modern, comfortable, and incredibly intense — a perfect stage for unforgettable matches.

Matches I attended

Matchdays in Qatar were electric. Short travel between venues meant more time with fans from everywhere, and every game felt like a festival inside and outside the stadiums.

Argentina vs. Mexico

Unforgettable atmosphere and sheer emotion.

Argentina vs. Poland

High stakes and world-class football.

Argentina vs. Netherlands

Dramatic night — pure World Cup magic.

Doha Corniche

Evening walks with the skyline glowing over the bay.

Souq Waqif

Traditional market, great food, and fan meetups.

Museum of Islamic Art

Beautiful architecture and a calm waterfront.

Trip Summary

Qatar 2022 felt unique: short distances, stunning stadiums, and a city that lived and breathed football all month long. It was the perfect mix of travel, culture, and unforgettable football moments.
