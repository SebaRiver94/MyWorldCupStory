Trip Summary

My journey through Russia for the 2018 FIFA World Cup was a once-in-a-lifetime adventure. I explored diverse cities, witnessed world-class football, and connected with fans from different cultures. It was a perfect combination of sports, travel, and friendship that will always hold a special place in my heart.
